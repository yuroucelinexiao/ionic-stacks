<h2 class="mb-12" style="font-size: 14pt;">
Electrochemistry uses renewable electricity to unlock reactions that would otherwise only occur at tremendously high temperatures and pressures. It is a powerful tool to tackle the climate crisis, from decarbonizing the air and oceans to providing an alternative pathway for sustainable fuel and mineral production.
